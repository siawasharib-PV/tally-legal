Privacy Policy — Tally

Effective date: 28 March 2026

Tally (“we”, “our”, “us”) helps you track and organize your financial records. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information (such as email address, if you create an account)
  • Financial records you enter (transactions, categories, notes, budgets)
  • Attachments you add (receipt photos/files)
  • Basic technical data needed for app operation and troubleshooting

2. How we use your information

  • Provide core app functionality (tracking, filtering, reporting)
  • Store and retrieve your records
  • Improve reliability and support
  • Respond to support requests

3. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with service providers required to operate the app (e.g., infrastructure/services), and only as needed.

4. Data retention

We keep your data while your account is active or as needed to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

5. Your controls

  • Access and update your data in the app
  • Request account/data deletion
  • Contact us for privacy requests

6. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we continuously improve protections.

7. Children’s privacy

Tally is not directed to children under 13 (or equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction).

8. International users

Your information may be processed in countries different from your own, subject to applicable safeguards and laws.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post updates on this page with a revised effective date.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact: tally.support.au@gmail.com
